-About Me-

Hi! My name is Darlena Tran. I am currently a freshman attending Dublin High School. Having two parents who both work in the engineering field, I have had a great deal of exposure to technology. I took computer development classes throughout my elementary school career and went on to explore different sites relating to programming, coding, and design. Working with various websites such as Scratch or MIT App Inventor, I learned to develop multiple computer languages, some of which being HTML and CSS, in order to form a finished, operating product. I grew fascinated with the process of making words come to life and began to take engineering courses to pursue those interests. Though I am not fully certain of the career path that I intend to follow, I intend to take multiple classes at Dublin High pertaining to the engineering and design fields in order to enhance my skills and experience.

Since participating in academic clubs, volunteer organizations, courses, and extracurricular activities, I have gained personal experience in qualities essential to school and everyday life such as time management and leadership. Through prior knowledge and classes like CSE, I have learned to be accurate, compassionate, open-minded, and meticulous with my work. I have a substantial understanding of how to conduct myself and how to collaborate efficiently with others. Because of past lessons and events in middle and high school, abilities I possess include patience and diligence to complete a set task and effectively propel a project forward.

Though I haven’t attended Dublin High for very long, I do have prior experience from my former middle school. During the course of the three years, I sustained a 4.0 grade point average and received multiple awards that include Mustang of the Month, honor roll, and principal’s honor roll. I participated in a significant volunteer organizations, the California Junior Scholarship Federation, and plan to participate in Dublin High’s academic club as well. Over the course of my membership, I assisted in the execution of fundraising and services, the most important being the conference honoring the association’s anniversary of ten years. In the first semester of high school, I have maintained a steady 4.0 GPA as well, taking classes such as Advanced English in order to challenge myself. I am currently a member of Key Club, Friends of the SPCA, Patch, and Drama Club.

Comprehensively, this website is my Engineering Portfolio and it is a compilation of my work and volunteer experience, my high school courses, personal information and engineering projects that I have been a part of.
